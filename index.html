Welcome Den Leader!

Here are your First Steps

  1. Fill out an Adult Application
    • And have it signed by your Pack's Chartered Organization Representative
  2. Complete Youth Protection Training
    1. Create an account on my.scouting.org and sign in
    2. Click the big red button on the right side of the home page
    3. Once complete, send certificate along with signed adult application to Gamehaven Council
  3. Complete 45-minute "Before your First Meeting" training
    1. Select "Den Leader Training"
    2. Click on "Enroll
    3. Click "Visit" on the second module, "Before Your First Meeting"
    4. Click "Start" on "CS-19 Welcome"
  4. Hold your First Den Meeting
  5. Complete 45-minute "Before Your First Outdoor Activity" Training
    1. Select "Den Leader Training"
    2. Click "Visit" on the third module, "Before your First Outdoor Activity"
    3. Click "Start" on "CS-19 Preparing Families for Outdoor Adventures"
  6. Hold your Second Den Meeting
  7. Complete 30-minute "Earning your Trained Strip" training
    1. Select "Den Leader Training
    2. Click "Visit" on the first module, "Earning your Trained Strip"
    3. Click "Start" on "CS-19 Involving Adults in Cub Scouting"
  8. Complete a Den Annual Plan
    1. Fit all required adventures into your annual calendar
    2. Fill empty spaces with elective adventures
  9. Register for Basic Adult Leader Outdoor Orientation
  10. Email completed documents (below) to brady.geary@scouting.org to receive a free "Trained" patch!
    • Certificate of Completion for Den Leader Training
    • Den Annual Plan
